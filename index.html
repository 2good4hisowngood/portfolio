About Me

As an Azure Architect with a focus on security, I am currently working towards my Master's degree in Information Security and Assurance through Embry-Riddle. I am constantly seeking new knowledge and opportunities to grow my skills.

Some notable achievements include:

  • Tasked with designing and implementing a managed cloud hosting platform for a legacy desktop application whose existing deployment process required coordination between several people, poor or non-existent documentation, and decoupled but dependent configuration, I designed and built automation pipelines with terraform, packer, and powershell to allow a single engineer to deploy all components seamlessly, reducing a process that often times took weeks down to hours.
